About us

The culture at Anamika is very focused on channeling all our collective energy into delivering high-impact customer solutions. Our policies and procedures align with strictly avoiding traditional organizational politics, and instead aligning our team's interests with the best interests of our clients.
To achieve this we have invested in creating an excellent work environment that ensures a great work-life balance.We strive hard to reach customer expectations and fulfill their dreams.We are proud of our team members and we strive to support growth and fulfillment for each one of them.

About Our Company

Anamikaa Infratech Private Limited is a Private company which is registered under the Ministry of Corporate affairs of the Government of India. The company is capable of catering to carry out and execute the following projects.

Providing consultancy services for all types of Government and Private projects and Governments tenders such as civil works, which include construction of roads, bridges, etc. for Government organizations like Indian Railways and NHAI (National Highway Authority Of India).
Real Estate - construction of residential buildings, apartments and consultancy.
